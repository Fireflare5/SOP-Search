


This SOP outlines the standardized process for purchasing supplies and subscriptions for CTE courses at Lindbergh High School, paid for with a combination of federal and state grants. It ensures compliance with grant regulations, budget adherence, and efficient procurement.

● Purchasing Procedure 
● Compliance and Documentation 
● Communication Plan 
● Resources 
CTE Department Chairs 
● Submits purchase requests based on instructional and operational needs. ● Ensures compliance with approved vendor lists and grant guidelines. 
● Completes CCQI, DESE required evaluation tool on a yearly basis to evaluate program needs. 
CTE Principal 
● Approves purchasing requests ensuring alignment with budget and grant compliance. ● Authorizes fund allocation and verifies necessity. 
● Oversees the goals of the CCQI ensuring budgets and planning match the goals of the program. 
Executive Director of Secondary Education 
● Reviews requests for compliance with grant regulations. 
● Ensures budget is planned/mapped out based on evidence and goals of the CCQI. ● Determines priority needs and wishlist needs.
● Allocates specific funds to specific departments or items (example: Perkins covers all Biomed and FACS supplies, Base and Performance covers all Tech and Engineering memberships, etc, 50/50 grant covers some of business supplies and some of marketing supplies). 
● Shares budget plans with appropriate parties for approval, cc’s T&L Administrative Assistant on correspondence as needed. 
● Enters final CTE Budget document with plan for academic year. 
● Creates July - June timeline for CTE purchasing (example: supplies needed prior to first day of school, supplies needed before Winter Break, supplies that need to wait until Spring). 
● Updates timeline as new requests come in from Department Chair throughout the academic year. 
● Ensures adherence to state and federal procurement regulations. 
● Communicates with DESE as needed. 
T&L Administrative Assistant 
● Verifies purchases align with grant funding restrictions. 
● Requests W9 documents from new vendors, compiles quotes provided by vendors and CTE department chairs. 
● Works with Executive Director of Business and Finance to move funds into accounts listed by Executive Director of Secondary Education on CTE Budget doc. ● Reviews Epegs accounts to ensure requested purchases match up with planned budgets. Working with Budget Amendments and FER reports. 
● Processes purchase orders and ensures proper documentation. 
● Completes purchases as directed. 
● Coordinates delivery with Lindbergh Schools Support Service Center, Library Processing, LHS Bookeeper, Department Chairs and teachers as needed. ● Monitors expenditures and reports compliance. 
● Directs new requests from teachers and Department Chairs to Executive Director of Secondary Education for consideration. 
● Maintains financial records. 
● Communicates with DESE as needed. 
1. Purchase Request Submission 
○ CTE teachers submit a purchase request form to their Department Chair. ○ Department Chair reviews and submits to the CTE Principal for approval. 2. Approval Process
○ Yearly budget planning is completed in April through meetings with the Exec. Dir. of Sec. Schools, CTE supervising Principal, and building Principal. 
○ Approvals will be determined by state funding. CTE departments will prioritize lists based on must haves, and wish list items. 
3. Compliance and Vendor Selection 
○ T&L Administrative Assistant verifies approved vendors and grant eligibility. ○ Ensures purchases comply with grant funding restrictions. 
4. Purchase Order Creation 
○ T&L Administrative Assistant generates and submits a purchase order (PO) to the vendor. 
○ Routing process to confirm funding compliance. 
5. Order Fulfillment and Receipt 
○ Vendor delivers supplies/subscriptions as per the PO. 
○ Staff receiving the order verifies accuracy and condition. 
6. Invoice Processing and Payment 
○ Vendor submits an invoice to T&L Administrative Assistant. 
○ Payment is processed following district financial procedures. 
7. Documentation and Record Keeping 
○ All records (POs, invoices, approvals) are maintained for audit purposes. ○ Grant Coordinator submits required reports. 
● All purchases must align with federal and state grant funding requirements. ● Documentation must be maintained for at least five years for audit purposes. ● Non-compliance may result in funding revocation or penalties. 
● SOP is shared by Executive Director of Secondary Education with all CTE staff via email and during CTE meetings. 
● Training sessions conducted annually for new and existing staff.
