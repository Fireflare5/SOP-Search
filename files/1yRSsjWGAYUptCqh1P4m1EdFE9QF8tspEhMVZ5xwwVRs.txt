


Identify the transition between district and building expenditures as new programs are developed 
● Chief Academic Officer - Oversees T and L budget 
● Executive Director of Secondary Education - Identifies programming budget needs ● Executive Director of Elementary Education - Identifies programming budget needs ● Principal - Oversees building budget 
● Initial Program Development - Start-up costs provided by T and L budget for the first two years of implementation; expansion of programs to additional grade spans may extend the life of T and L budget support 
● Ongoing Program Costs - After full implementation, supplies are provided in the building budget unless provided via Perkins funding 
The curriculum cycle updates will be funded through the Teaching and Learning budget 
When discussing new program development, ensure communication of this process.
