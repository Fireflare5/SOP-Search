


This SOP outlines the process for meeting Missouri DESE Title I regulations regarding: ● Annual Title I Parent Meetings 
● Title I Consultations with Stakeholders 
● Family Engagement Surveys 
● Parent and Family Involvement Activities 
Executive Director of Elementary Education 
● Ensure compliance with Missouri DESE Title I regulations. 
● Schedule and facilitate annual meetings and stakeholder consultations. ● Collect and analyze parent feedback from surveys. 
● Maintain documentation for federal program monitoring. 
Building Principals 
● Collaborate with the Executive Director of Elementary Education and Chief Academic Officer to schedule meetings. 
● Plan Fall annual meeting; invite and communicate events with families. ● Distribute and promote family engagement surveys. 
● Recruit and invite parents to participate in stakeholder meetings. 
● Ensure meaningful family engagement opportunities are implemented at the school level. 
Chief Academic Officer 
● Organizes and conducts yearly annual stakeholder meeting 
● Submits Title I application and budget
● Communicates any budget allocations to the Executive Director of Elementary Education ● Oversees compliance and tiered monitoring 
Classroom Teachers 
● Encourage parent participation in meetings and surveys. 
● Assist with dissemination of information and invitations. 
Parent Liaison 
● Serve as a communication bridge between the school and families. 
● Participate in annual stakeholder meetings. 
● Assist with organizing and interpreting parent feedback. 
Annual Title I Parent Meetings (Fall and Spring) 
● Held at the beginning of the school year (Fall) and second semester (Stakeholders). ● Parents are informed of the school's participation in Title I, Title I requirements, and the right of parents to be involved. 
● Agenda includes curriculum overview, assessments, proficiency levels, and opportunities for involvement. 
● Meeting invitations distributed via multiple channels: school website, newsletters, automated calls, flyers. Title I must be in the name of the event. 
● Documentation includes agenda, sign-in sheets to include role, meeting minutes, and presentation slides. 
Annual Stakeholders Meeting 
● Conducted at least annually to gather input on Title I program planning and implementation. 
● Stakeholders include parents, teachers, administrators, and community members. ● Feedback used to review and revise Schoolwide/Targeted Assistance Plans, Parent and Family Engagement Policy, and School-Parent Compact. 
● Document input and revisions in meeting summaries. 
Surveys 
● Parent engagement surveys distributed at least once annually (recommended in spring). ● Surveys include questions on communication, involvement, satisfaction, and recommendations.
● Results are compiled, analyzed, and shared with staff and families. ● Data informs future engagement efforts and Title I planning. 
Parent and Family Involvement Activities 
● Activities are planned collaboratively with parents based on feedback and student needs. 
● May include literacy nights, math games, parent workshops, and student showcases. ● Attendance tracked; feedback forms distributed when possible. 
● Activities reflect goals in the School Improvement Plan and support student achievement. 
Who: All families of Title I students, school staff, district Title I personnel What: Purpose of meetings, survey distribution, and involvement opportunities When: 
● Fall: Annual meeting, initial parent communications 
● Spring: Survey distribution and follow-up meeting 
● Ongoing: Regular reminders, invitations, updates via newsletters and social media, district website
