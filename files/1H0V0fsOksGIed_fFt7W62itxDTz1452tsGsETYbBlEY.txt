


Purpose 
This SOP outlines the process for evaluating Secondary Career and Technical Education (CTE) programs using the Missouri Department of Elementary and Secondary Education (DESE) Common Criteria and Quality Indicators (CCQI) Tool. This ensures programs remain compliant, relevant, and focused on continuous improvement. 
This SOP includes: 
● Responsibilities 
● Evaluation Procedures 
● Improvement Planning 
● Communication Plan 
● Resources 
Responsibilities 
CTE Instructors 
● Maintain program documentation (curriculum, lesson plans, assessment data) ● Participate in evaluations and provide evidence of implementation 
● Support improvement actions identified in the CCQI process 
Building Administrators
● Ensure annual CCQI evaluations are completed 
● Collaborate with instructional staff to review and approve improvement plans ● Monitor implementation of improvement strategies 
Advisory Committee Members 
● Participate in reviewing program goals, curriculum, and industry relevance ● Provide feedback and recommendations for program improvement 
Procedure 
Conduct Annual Program Evaluation 
● Use the DESE CCQI rubric to assess all six criteria: 
1. Programs of Study 
2. Curriculum 
3. Instruction 
4. Assessment 
5. Career and Technical Student Organizations (CTSOs) 
6. Program Management and Planning 
● Evaluate each quality indicator using the 4-point rubric scale Collect Required Documentation 
● Use Appendix A of the CCQI manual to gather evidence such as: ○ Advisory committee meeting minutes 
○ Curriculum guides and revision logs 
○ Dual enrollment agreements 
○ CTSO programs of work and membership logs
○ Industry-recognized credential (IRC) results 
○ Work-based learning records 
○ Program budgets and professional development logs 
Score and Analyze 
● Assign rubric levels (1–4) based on evidence 
● Use scores to identify strengths and areas for growth 
Develop an Improvement Plan 
● Use Appendix B to document action steps, target dates, and responsible parties 
● Share with administration and advisory committee for feedback and support Submit Reports 
● Submit completed CCQI evaluation tool and documentation to DESE according to the program submission schedule 
Section 4 Communication Plan 
Communication Plan 
● Annual results will be reviewed with: 
○ CTE instructors 
○ Building administrators 
○ Advisory committees 
● Final improvement plans will be shared during curriculum review or department meetings
● Results may be incorporated into district-wide school improvement plans
