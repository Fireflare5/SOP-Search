


In order for Project Lead the Way (PLTW) teachers and students to access online PLTW resources, the STEM Programs Coordinator must sync class rosters in August using their myPLTW account. 
● Executive Director of Secondary Education - Oversees secondary programs, including PLTW 
● STEM Program Coordinator - Syncs class rosters on the myPLTW website. 
● Log in - In early August, after Infinite Campus and Classlink have synced, prior to the start of the school year, the STEM Programs Coordinator logs into pltw.org. ● Syncing classes 
a. Select My Sites 
b. Select a school 
c. Select Import Clever Classrooms 
d. Verify all imported classes, teachers, and sections 
i. contact the district Clever manager if the sync is not accurate 
Two days after syncing, notify teachers and the Executive Director of Secondary Education that PLTW classes are synced. Ask teachers to log into myPLTW to verify their rosters for accuracy.
