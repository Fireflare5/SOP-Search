


This SOP outlines the process for teachers and staff at Lindbergh Schools to request approval and funding from the Teaching & Learning (T&L) Department to attend professional learning conferences. The process ensures: 
● Equitable access to professional learning opportunities. 
● Compliance with budgetary and policy guidelines. 
● A streamlined approval workflow. 
● Teachers & Staff: Identify relevant conferences and submit funding requests. ● School Principals: Review and endorse requests before submission. ● T&L Department: Evaluate requests based on alignment with district goals and budget availability. 
● Conference Selection & Request Submission 
a. Teachers and staff identify a relevant professional learning conference. b. Complete the district’s Professional Learning Request Form. 
c. Submit the form to the school principal for initial review. 
d. Dir. of Elem Ed will approve after the building principal 
● Principal Endorsement & Central Office Submission 
a. The principal reviews the request for alignment with school and district priorities. b. Approved requests are forwarded to the T&L Department at the Central Office. ● T&L Department Review & Funding Approval 
a. The T&L Department evaluates requests based on professional development objectives and budget constraints. 
b. Requests that meet criteria are approved, and applicants are notified. 
c. The Finance Office is informed for payment processing. 
● Conference Attendance & Reimbursement
a. Approved attendees, with the help of appropriate Administrative Assistant, make necessary travel and registration arrangements. Typically budget determines the appropriate Administrative Assistant. 
b. Submit receipts and required documentation for reimbursement within the designated time frame. 
c. The Finance Office processes reimbursements as per district policy. 
● The SOP is shared with all school staff annually via email and staff meetings. ● Training sessions are provided to ensure compliance with the process. ● Updates are communicated through district-wide announcements.
