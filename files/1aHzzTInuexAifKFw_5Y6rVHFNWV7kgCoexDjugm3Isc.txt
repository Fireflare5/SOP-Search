


This SOP provides a clear process for teachers experiencing access issues with digital curriculum resources across the Pre-K – 12 public classrooms in our school district. It ensures prompt troubleshooting, effective communication, and consistent resolution. This SOP covers: 
● Basic troubleshooting steps 
● The reporting pathway for unresolved issues 
● Escalation and documentation 
● Follow-up responsibilities 
Teachers 
● Perform initial troubleshooting 
● Document and report issues to their Department Chair or Grade-Level Leader 
Department Chairs / Grade-Level Leaders 
● Assist teachers with verification and documentation 
● Report unresolved issues to the Technology Department 
● Monitor issue trends at the team/grade level 
Technology Department 
● Troubleshoot technical access issues 
● Coordinate with vendors or platforms as needed 
● Provide status updates to team leaders and administrative staff 
Teaching & Learning Administrative Assistant
● Maintain a log of access issues district-wide 
● Communicate with curriculum leaders and Technology Department regarding unresolved or recurring issues 
● Provide regular status reports to the Teaching & Learning Department 
Initial Troubleshooting by Teacher: 
● Refresh browser or try another browser 
● Clear cache/cookies 
● Restart device and verify internet access 
● Confirm login credentials are correct 
Check Known Issues: 
● Review communication from vendor 
● Check shared troubleshooting documents or training notes 
Document the Issue: 
● Take screenshots of the issue or error message 
● Note platform name, student email, date/time, device used, and steps already taken Report to Department Chair or Grade-Level Leader: 
● Share documentation and describe how it’s impacting instruction 
● Leaders provide initial support or validate issue 
Escalate to Technology Department: 
● Department Chair or Grade-Level Leader submits ticket to the Technology Helpdesk ● Include teacher’s documentation, urgency level, and affected digital platform 
Notify Teaching & Learning Administrative Assistant: 
● Once submitted to the Technology Department, Department Chair/Grade-Level Leader informs the Teaching & Learning Administrative Assistant 
● Assistant logs the issue for department-wide tracking 
Follow-Up: 
● The Technology Department provides resolution status to the Department Chair
● If no update in 48 hours, the issue is re-flagged by the Chair or Assistant ● Teachers should be updated promptly once resolved 
● This SOP will be introduced during staff onboarding and digital curriculum training sessions 
● Available on the district intranet and shared in beginning-of-year documents ● Department Chairs and Grade-Level Leaders will review the SOP during initial team meetings
