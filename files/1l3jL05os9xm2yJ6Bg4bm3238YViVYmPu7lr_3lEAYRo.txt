


Outline the requirements and process for approving volunteer drivers to transport students for Lindbergh Schools. It ensures compliance with district policies and safety standards. The SOP covers:
● Eligibility requirements for volunteer drivers (employees, parents, and community members) 
● Documentation and approval process 
● Vehicle safety requirements 
● Parent authorization requirements 
● Communication and oversight responsibilities 
Volunteer Drivers (Employees, Parents, and Community Members): 
● At no time should an adult be alone in a vehicle with a student. 
● Submit required documentation prior to transporting students. 
● Ensure compliance with all vehicle safety requirements. 
● Obtain proper authorization before transporting students. 
Event Sponsors/Administrators: 
● Communicate requirements to volunteer drivers. 
● Collect and verify documentation before approving volunteer drivers. 
● Ensure parent authorization is obtained for each student transported. 
Human Resources Department: 
● Process volunteer background checks for non-employee drivers. 
● Maintain records of approved volunteer driver
District Office: (who? See additional questions at the bottom) 
● Review and approve submitted documentation. 
● Notify volunteer drivers and event sponsors of approval status. 
● Ensure compliance with district policies regarding student transportation. 
1. Eligibility Requirements 
For Employees: 
● Must submit the following before transporting students: 
○ Copy of a valid driver's license. 
○ Proof of vehicle insurance (driver’s insurance serves as primary coverage). ○ Driver record without personal information from the Missouri Department of Revenue (required every 3 years). 
○ Photos of: 
■ Vehicle to be used for transportation. 
■ License plate with registration sticker. 
■ Safety inspection sticker or odometer reading (if the vehicle is under 10 years old and has fewer than 150,000 miles). 
For Parents and Community Members: 
● Must have a volunteer background check on file with the HR department (valid for 3 years, reimbursable). 
● Must submit the same documentation as employees listed above. ● The event sponsor is responsible for ensuring all documentation is submitted and approved before transportation occurs. 
2. Approval Process 
1. Volunteer drivers submit required documentation to the designated event sponsor. 2. Event sponsor reviews and submits documentation to the district office. 3. The District Office reviews submissions and notifies the driver and event sponsor upon approval. 
4. Approval does not authorize transportation until parent authorization is received. 3. Parent Authorization Requirement 
● A signed permission slip must be obtained before transporting any student. ● If a signed form is not feasible, documentation of parental consent (email, text, or phone call) must be recorded by a district employee and retained in school records. ● The safest approach is to require a signed permission slip for each student.
4. Vehicle Safety Requirements 
● The vehicle must not exceed nine-passenger capacity. 
● All passengers must have a seatbelt. 
● The vehicle must pass a safety inspection or meet alternative criteria (odometer reading requirements). 
● Volunteer driver requirements will be communicated via principals to coaches, sponsors and event planners. 
● Event sponsors are responsible for ensuring compliance and providing clear instructions to volunteers. 
● HR and District Office will provide periodic reminders about renewal requirements for background checks and driver records.
